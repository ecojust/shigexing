id='poem_sentence_106_1148823_comment'></div>曰：「众生应无分耶」？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_107_1148823_comment'></div>师曰：「我为众生说」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_108_1148823_comment'></div>曰：「某甲聋瞽不闻，师应得闻」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_109_1148823_comment'></div>师曰：「我亦不闻」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_110_1148823_comment'></div>曰：「师既不闻，争知无情说法」？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_111_1148823_comment'></div>师曰：「我若得闻，即齐诸佛，汝即不闻我所说法」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_112_1148823_comment'></div>曰：「众生毕竟得闻否」？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_113_1148823_comment'></div>师曰：「众生若闻，即非众生」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_114_1148823_comment'></div>国师之答，稍有深致，识者知之。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_115_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_116_1148823'>七问：世间有情变为无情，如石夫人、地蟾、石蟹等。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_116_1148823_comment'></div>无情变为有情，如麰麦、腐草化为飞蝶、萤火等。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_117_1148823_comment'></div>此等生类，是何因缘而尔迁变耶？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_118_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_119_1148823'>答：只一如来藏性，众生循业发现不同，是故山河大地建立，胎卵湿化成形。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_119_1148823_comment'></div>如《佛顶经》曰：「觉海性澄圆，圆澄觉元妙。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_120_1148823_comment'></div>元明照生所，所立照性亡。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_121_1148823_comment'></div>迷妄有虚空，依空立世界。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_122_1148823_comment'></div>想澄成国土，知觉乃众生」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_123_1148823_comment'></div>且无情化有情者，神识乘时而来，依草附木也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_124_1148823_comment'></div>有情化无情者，神识遇气迁变，驱㲉枯朽也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_125_1148823_comment'></div>当知千变不离于一念，万化难逃于藏识。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_126_1148823_comment'></div>世间更有火鼠、汤虫、冰蚕、石燕，情器难量，业报奚测。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_127_1148823_comment'></div>佛及众生俱不思议，于兹见矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_128_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_129_1148823'>八问：世间有人，此身未死，彼处已生，如王院主等。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_129_1148823_comment'></div>此何因缘也？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_130_1148823_comment'></div>若云众生业用自在者，一切众生皆有业用，何故例无此事耶？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_131_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_132_1148823'>答：天台承南岳三种妙法，一曰众生法，二曰佛法，三曰心法。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_132_1148823_comment'></div>此三妙，故不可以牛羊之眼观视，亦不可以凡情世智测识，有如来智眼者方堪量度。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_133_1148823_comment'></div>良由体性不可思议，一一高广，故《华严》所谓「如心佛亦尔，如佛众生然。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_134_1148823_comment'></div>心佛及众生，是三无差别」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_135_1148823_comment'></div>诸佛妙證无差之道，故能于一身化千百亿身，于一法演无量诸法，六根三业，法界大用，无有穷尽。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_136_1148823_comment'></div>众生处迷，虽不能尔，而由性德本具此体用，故只于业报之身亦能少分变现。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_137_1148823_comment'></div>如上古舜帝，分身而应二妃；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_138_1148823_comment'></div>倩女离魂，而合为一质；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_139_1148823_comment'></div>县令昼寝，识化为鱼；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_140_1148823_comment'></div>至有二人双存枕上，二魂悉在地狱受殃者。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_141_1148823_comment'></div>如斯等事，不能殚举，皆是众生界中业报难思之事。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_142_1148823_comment'></div>是知不独王院主一人而已，何苦兴兹疑难哉？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_143_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_144_1148823'>九问：有情无情既同一真性，何故斫伐树木，不为冤对，不令人随地狱？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_144_1148823_comment'></div>才损有情，便为冤对，令人堕苦受报，何耶？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_145_1148823_comment'></div>又复既是依正二报同一法性，何故有情有觉知，无情无觉知耶？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_146_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_147_1148823'>答：法界体性，离诸妄念。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_147_1148823_comment'></div>相等虚空，无所不遍。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_148_1148823_comment'></div>遍依遍正，遍根遍尘。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_149_1148823_comment'></div>至一极微，无非法界，全体而遍。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_150_1148823_comment'></div>嗟夫群生，梏桎斯体，妄为情器。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_151_1148823_comment'></div>情则四生六道，水陆空行，器则山川岳渎，草木丛林。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_152_1148823_comment'></div>于情分中妄计我人知见，故有苦楚冤对之事。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_153_1148823_comment'></div>器分既无我人知见，故无业累雠偿之愆。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_154_1148823_comment'></div>如是不同，皆是众生境界虚妄分别，究论法界，体性有何差别？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_155_1148823_comment'></div>故荆溪曰：「纵然造依造正，依理终无异辙」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_156_1148823_comment'></div>斯言是也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_157_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_158_1148823'>十问：一切众生既同一觉性，何故捶打他人，不觉痛苦，却为快乐？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_158_1148823_comment'></div>既各不相知，云何辨平等觉性耶？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_159_1148823_comment'></div></div><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_160_1148823'>答：清净本然，尚无一法可以当情，况论捶打痛苦之事？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_160_1148823_comment'></div>但真如不守自性，变为诸法，如一源水，从流派别，清浊动静，海咸河淡。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_161_1148823_comment'></div>是故于中人我竞起，爱恶是兴，以强陵弱，攻击不休。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_162_1148823_comment'></div>子今设问，正是铨量迷妄中事，惟当返妄归真，背尘合觉。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_163_1148823_comment'></div>则了十方三世，一切众生，同共一法身，一身一智，慧力无畏亦然。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_164_1148823_comment'></div>到此之时，一證平等觉性，尚无彼此色相迭相见，岂复论于世间捶打胜负哉！</div><div
