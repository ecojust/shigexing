id='poem_sentence_8_1214199_comment'></div>自周室板荡，宣王拨乱反正，其《诗》美之，小有《吉日》、《鸿雁》，大有《崧高》、《蒸民》，不幸继以幽王，而骊山之祸作焉。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_9_1214199_comment'></div>然而文武之泽未殄也，故平王东迁，人犹望其复兴也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_10_1214199_comment'></div>及其久也，政益衰，法益坏，《黍离》变为国风，陵迟极矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_11_1214199_comment'></div>方是时也，去文王已五百馀岁矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_12_1214199_comment'></div>冠屦颠倒，夷狄乱华，天生圣人，又不见用。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_13_1214199_comment'></div>《春秋》于此时，傥不复作，天下不胥为夷狄禽兽者，吾不敢信也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_14_1214199_comment'></div>故夫子因鲁史一十二公，始隐终麟，以二百四十年之事，创为一代之典，善善而恶恶，是是而非非，宽不慢，猛不残，文不华，实不陋，久而弥光，可以垂后世，传无穷，真后王之懿范也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_15_1214199_comment'></div>所谓考诸三王而不谬，百世以俟圣人而不惑者，其此书之谓乎！<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_16_1214199_comment'></div>或者曰：「《春秋》其事则桓、文，孔子成《春秋》而乱臣贼子惧，其信然乎」？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_17_1214199_comment'></div>曰：《春秋》自隐公以来，征伐四出，盟会纷然，迨庄历僖，楚人大为中国患。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_18_1214199_comment'></div>于时尊天子，攘夷狄，使天下不遂左衽者，桓文二公之力也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_19_1214199_comment'></div>故伐楚之役，齐桓称爵；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_20_1214199_comment'></div>城濮之战，文公以霸。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_21_1214199_comment'></div>自后世言之，二公之功烈莫盛焉；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_22_1214199_comment'></div>自三王之时言之，不免为罪也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_23_1214199_comment'></div>首止之会、河阳之狩是也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_24_1214199_comment'></div>夫子因其事而辞之，以明王道，故曰《春秋》其事则桓、文。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_25_1214199_comment'></div>古之圣人能以天下为一家，中国为一人者，非有甚高难行之行、卓异之术也，君君臣臣、父父子子，而天下治矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_26_1214199_comment'></div>《书》曰：「天叙有典，敕我五典五惇哉！<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_27_1214199_comment'></div>天秩有礼，自我五礼有庸哉」！<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_28_1214199_comment'></div>盖典也礼也，皆天也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_29_1214199_comment'></div>尧舜之治天下，不越乎君臣父子之间，而礼以文之者也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_30_1214199_comment'></div>故《春秋》诛一世子止，而天下之为人子者莫敢不孝；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_31_1214199_comment'></div>戮一大夫盾，而天下之为人臣者莫敢不忠。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_32_1214199_comment'></div>故曰孔子成《春秋》而乱臣贼子惧。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_33_1214199_comment'></div>孟氏之言，抑有由也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_34_1214199_comment'></div>或曰：「孔子删《诗》、《书》，定《礼》、《乐》，赞《易》道，三王之道，尽于此矣，而又作《春秋》，何也」？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_35_1214199_comment'></div>曰：五经论其理，《春秋》见之行事，《春秋》圣人之用也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_36_1214199_comment'></div>龟山尝语人曰：「《春秋》其事之终欤」！<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_37_1214199_comment'></div>学者先明五经，然后学《春秋》，则其用利矣，亦以此也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_38_1214199_comment'></div>久矣哉，春秋之掩于传注也！<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_39_1214199_comment'></div>犹鉴掩于尘，不有人焉，刮垢磨光以还其明，则是后之学者将终不睹圣人之心，天下生灵将终不见三代之治，而夫子生平之志将终不行。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_40_1214199_comment'></div>理必无是也，此伊川之所以有《春秋传》也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_41_1214199_comment'></div>近世说《春秋》者多矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_42_1214199_comment'></div>政和岁在丁酉，余从龟山先生于毗陵，授学经年，尽裒得其书以归，惟《春秋传》未之获睹也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_43_1214199_comment'></div>宣和之初，自辇下趋郏鄏，门人尹焞出以授予。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_44_1214199_comment'></div>退而考，合于经，验之以心，而参之以古今之学，盖其所得者十五六。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_45_1214199_comment'></div>于《春秋》大义，譬如日月经天，河海带地，莫不昭然。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_46_1214199_comment'></div>微词妙旨，譬如玑衡之察，时有所见。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_47_1214199_comment'></div>用是掇其至当者，作《指归》。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_48_1214199_comment'></div>又因前人纂集之功，分别条章，裁成义例者，作《释例》。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_49_1214199_comment'></div>未知中否？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_50_1214199_comment'></div>要须雍容自尽于燕閒静一之中，迟之以岁月，积之以力久，优而游之，使自求之，餍而饫之，使自趋之，则于《春秋》之学，其庶几乎！</div><div id='poem_sentence_51_1214199_comment'></div></div></div><br /><a href="javascript: ViewDetail('scope=&author=&beginYear=1118&endYear=1118')">1118年春节后</a>　拜别杨时。<br /></div>
-----end



-----title
尤溪
-----detail
<div class='label1' id='1ac0c44dce8841b8a33c2f26cedb5774_label'><a href='javascript: DetailSwitch("1ac0c44dce8841b8a33c2f26cedb5774");'><span id='1ac0c44dce8841b8a33c2f26cedb5774_link' class='switch'>—</span></a><a href="javascript: ViewDetail('scope=&author=&beginYear=1124&endYear=1124')">1124年</a>，53岁</div><div id='1ac0c44dce8841b8a33c2f26cedb5774' class='detail'><a href="javascript: ViewDetail('scope=&author=&beginYear=1124&endYear=1124')">1124年</a>　应学生朱松邀请，赴尤溪为韦斋作记。作品：《韦斋记》<span class='inlineComment2'>（六年后朱熹生于此屋。）</span><div id='poem_1214201' class='_poem'><div id='poem_title_1214201' class='poemTitle showDetail'><a href='/Writing/1214201?labeling=true' target='_blank'>韦斋记</a><span class='authorDate'>（1124年）</span><span class='poemAuthor'><span class='inlineComment1'> 宋 · </span><a href='javascript: ShowPoemAuthorProfile(30981, "宋", "罗从彦", "poem_title_1214201")'>罗从彦</a></span><br /><div class='titleIndent'></div></div><div id='poem_title_1214201_comment'></div><div class='poemContent' id='poem_content_1214201'><div class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_0_1214201'>宣和三年岁在癸卯之中秋，朱乔年得尤溪尉，尝治一室，聚群书，宴坐寝休其间。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_0_1214201_comment'></div>后知《大学》之渊源，异端之学无所入于其心，自知卞急害道，名其室曰韦斋，取古人韦佩之义。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_1_1214201_comment'></div>泛观古人有以物为戒者，有以人为戒者，所谓佩韦以物为戒者也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_2_1214201_comment'></div>人之大患在于不知过，知过而思自改，于是有戒焉。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_3_1214201_comment'></div>非贤者孰能之乎？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_4_1214201_comment'></div>予始以困掩未能遂志，因作舫斋陆海中，且思古人所以进此道者，必有由而然。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_5_1214201_comment'></div>久之，乃喟然叹曰：自孟轲氏没，更历汉唐，寥寥千载，迄无其人有能自树立者。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_6_1214201_comment'></div>不过注心于外，崇尚世儒之语而已。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_7_1214201_comment'></div>与之游孔氏之门，入于尧舜之道，其必不能至矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_8_1214201_comment'></div>夫《中庸》之书，世之学者尽心以知性，躬行以尽性者也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_9_1214201_comment'></div>而其始则曰：「喜怒哀乐之未发谓之中」。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_12_1214201_comment'></div>此言何谓也？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_13_1214201_comment'></div>差之毫釐，谬以千里，《大学》之道「在知所止」而已。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_14_1214201_comment'></div>苟知所止，则知学之先后；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_15_1214201_comment'></div>不知所止，则于学无自而进矣。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_16_1214201_comment'></div>漆雕开之学曰「吾斯之未能信」，曾点之学曰「异乎三子者之撰」，颜渊之学曰「回虽不敏，请事斯语矣」，而孔子悦开与点，称颜回以庶几，盖许其进也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_17_1214201_comment'></div>此予之所尝自勉者也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_18_1214201_comment'></div>故以圣贤则莫学而非道，以俗学则莫学而非物。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_19_1214201_comment'></div>乔年才高而智明，其刚不屈于俗，其学也方进而未艾。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_20_1214201_comment'></div>斋成之明年，使人来求记于余。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_21_1214201_comment'></div>余辞以不能，则非朋友之义；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_22_1214201_comment'></div>欲蹈袭世儒之语，则非吾心。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_23_1214201_comment'></div>故以其常所自勉者并书之，使人知其在此而不在彼也。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_24_1214201_comment'></div>或曰：「韦斋之作，终无益于学也邪」？<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_25_1214201_comment'></div>曰：古之人固有刻诸盘杅，铭诸几杖，置金人以戒多言，置欹器以戒自满，圣人皆有取焉。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_26_1214201_comment'></div>苟善取之，则韦斋之作不无补也。</div><div id='poem_sentence_27_1214201_comment'></div></div></div><br /></div>
