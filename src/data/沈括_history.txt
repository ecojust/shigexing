class='articleSentence' id='poem_sentence_0_1182547'>臣某言：伏蒙圣恩，以臣投进《守令图》了毕，特支赐绢一百匹，仍许任便居住者。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_0_1182547_comment'></div>缺简程工，方虞于速谴；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_1_1182547_comment'></div>匪颁将命，遽被于过恩。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_2_1182547_comment'></div>祗荷宽矜，伏深震惧。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_3_1182547_comment'></div>臣某<span class='inlineComment2'>（中谢。）</span>切念臣材不济务，识后常伦。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_4_1182547_comment'></div>顷缘误恩，谬职边琐。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_5_1182547_comment'></div>兴师忽生于裨将，护筑旋隶于王人。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_6_1182547_comment'></div>弱羽易摧，一身甘俟于夷灭；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_7_1182547_comment'></div>大明委照，万死曲荷于存全。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_8_1182547_comment'></div>更缘方域之謏闻，得返渔樵之旧业。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_9_1182547_comment'></div>屡阽死所，岂斑白之敢期；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_10_1182547_comment'></div>垂老馀年，皆朝廷之所赐。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_11_1182547_comment'></div>此盖伏遇太皇太后陛下恩同天地，施及刍莱。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_12_1182547_comment'></div>出处既原其本心，功过亦容以相补。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_13_1182547_comment'></div>悯系匏之不食，开生路于已衰。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_14_1182547_comment'></div>听其释佐吏之拘，使亲得庶民之事。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_15_1182547_comment'></div>步归故里，敢忘畎亩之心；<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_16_1182547_comment'></div>回望宸廷，犹深犬马之恋。<div class='inline' id='poem_sentence_17_1182547_comment'></div>瞻望阙庭，臣某无任感天荷圣激切屏营之至，谨奉表称谢以闻。</div><div id='poem_sentence_18_1182547_comment'></div></div></div><br /></div>
